Vanilla Cupcakes

Description

These are the easiest most delicious vanilla cupcakes you'll ever need to whip up. Wheather its for a quick snack or for 4 o'clock tea with the family.This recipe is going to have everone talking.

The milk is the star of this show.For that reason you cannot omit it or substitute it. This vanilla cupcake butter can also be added chocolate chips,or blueberries.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Baking Powder
  • Eggs
  • Unsalted Butter

Steps

  1. Pre-heat your oven to 350 degrees Farenheight.
  2. Place the cupcake holders in the cupcake baking tray.
  3. In a large bowl with a hand mixer beat the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add in the eggs one by one beating them until they are well incorparated.
  5. In a seperate bowl seive the flour and baking powder.
  6. Add in the flour and milk in alernate intervals making sure you begin and end with the flour.
  7. Pour the batter in the cupcake holders using a medium sized icecream scoop.
  8. Bake for 20-25 minutes.
  9. Remove from tin after they have completely cooled.

    10. Serve with a cold glass of milk!
