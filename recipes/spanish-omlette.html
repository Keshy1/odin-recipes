Spanish=Omlette

Description

This will be the easiest spanish omlette you'll ever prepare. The ingredients are simple and are very readily available in your supermarket or farmer's market near you.

The Spanish Paprika is the star of the show. It gives just the right amount of heat and smokiness to the omlette. If you're not a fan of heat you can reduce the amount of spanish paprika you put in but do not completly omit it. You'll thank me later.:-)

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Green Cupsicum
  • Yellow Cupsicum
  • Red cupsicum
  • Red onions
  • Cooking oil
  • Fresh Corriander
  • Spanish Paprika
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs together with the spanish paprika and black pepper until smooth.
  2. Chop all the cupsicum into tiny bits.
  3. Add in the capsicum bits to the egg mixture and whisk till they are equally distributed.
  4. Chop the onion into semi circles.
  5. In a heated non stick pan pour a tablespoon of oil. Then add the onions. Saute the onions.
  6. Add in the egg mixture.Make sure its equally distributed in the pan.
  7. Reduce the heat to medium low and cover the pan.
  8. Cook for 3 minutes then flip over,cover the pan and cook for 2 minutes.
  9. Plate it and garnish with a leaf or two of corriander. Serve whilst hot.
