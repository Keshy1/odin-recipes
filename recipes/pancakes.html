Pancakes

Description

This recipe will give you the most fluffy pancakes you could ever dream off. They are airy on the inside and have just enough crunch on the outside

The Buttermilk is the star ingredient in this recipe.You could substitute it with milk or even water but I would highly suggest you use the butter milk. They are so fluffy you dont even need to use maple syrup or any other kind of syrup,feel free to use some,just know its optional.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Butter milk
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Butter or oil

Steps

  1. Pour the butter milk into a container.
  2. Add the baking powder and eggs and whisk.
  3. whisk in the flour.
  4. Add the sugar.
  5. Melt the butter on a heated pan.
  6. Pour in the pancake batter a little at a time.
  7. Once you see bubbles forming flip the pancake.
  8. Serve the pancakes when hot
